Community Issue

Our issue is sex education in high schools. Today's high school students are not well equipped with knowledge on practicing safe-sex and how not to succumb to the pressures put on them to have sex. This lack of a proper sex education in high schools plays a factor in an increasing number of teens having unprotected sex. We aim to introduce a new mindset of valuing safe-sex to high school students.

About

Generation Citizen works to ensure that every student in the U.S. receives an effective action civics education, which provides them with the knowledge and skills necessary to participate in our democracy as active citizens. As a part of Generation, high school seniors in the Brooklyn High School for Law and Technology chose to work on a community issue that they believed needed attention.

A survey of students revealed that there is a lot of BETTER SEX ED is needed among our peers.

Project

To Convince the Department of Education to dedicate an entire course curriculum to effective Sex Education - with a structured curriculum and experienced best teaching practices.
